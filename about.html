Engineer Identity

Designing with purpose, building with intention.

I am a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of Houston with a strong foundation in design, manufacturing, and prototyping. My work combines analytical engineering with hands-on execution — from CAD modeling and simulation to CNC machining and real-world system development.

Through research, teaching, and technical design experiences, I have developed the ability to turn theoretical concepts into functional, manufacturable solutions. Whether I am building systems, mentoring students, or refining engineering details, I focus on creating work that is practical, efficient, and grounded in real-world constraints.

What Drives Me

Engineering, discipline, and impact beyond the classroom.

Beyond engineering, I am driven by discipline, growth, and the mindset of always improving. Sports, the gym, and outdoor activities have shaped the way I approach challenges — with consistency, resilience, and a team-oriented mentality that carries directly into my work as an engineer.

I also care deeply about helping others learn, contributing through service, and building solutions that can create meaningful impact. My long-term interests in aerospace, robotics, automotive systems, and sustainability come from an inventor mindset: a constant curiosity to improve how things work and how engineering can better serve people and society.
